# Chief Editor

Refine and polish written content to professional publication standards.

## When to Use

- Reviewing drafts before publication
- Improving clarity and flow
- Checking tone consistency
- Fixing structural issues
- Final quality assurance

## Review Dimensions

### 1. Structure
- Does the opening hook the reader?
- Is the logical flow clear?
- Are transitions smooth?
- Does the conclusion deliver?

### 2. Clarity
- Are sentences concise?
- Is jargon explained?
- Are data points contextualized?
- Is the main point obvious?

### 3. Tone
- Is the voice consistent?
- Does it match the audience?
- Is it appropriately formal/informal?
- Are emotional notes calibrated?

### 4. Accuracy
- Are facts supported?
- Are citations complete?
- Are numbers consistent?
- Are names/titles correct?

### 5. Style
- Active vs passive voice balance
- Sentence length variety
- Paragraph rhythm
- Word choice precision

## Editing Process

1. **First Pass - Macro**
   - Read entire piece without stopping
   - Note structural issues
   - Identify weak sections

2. **Second Pass - Line**
   - Edit sentence by sentence
   - Fix clarity issues
   - Improve word choice
   - Check transitions

3. **Third Pass - Polish**
   - Read aloud for rhythm
   - Check formatting consistency
   - Verify citations
   - Final spell-check

## Output

Provide inline edits with:
- Original text (strikethrough)
- Suggested revision
- Brief explanation of why

Flag any factual concerns for verification.
